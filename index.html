  • By holding the eyeball, and chanting

    "How much wood would a woodchuck chuck

    if a woodchuck could chuck wood?

    A woodchuck would chuck all the wood he could

    if a woodchuck could chuck wood."

    The amulet's glow increases with every word - nearly blinding you. You realise if you chant it 3 times you will enter a timeloop (because magic) and restart your quest to fight criminals. However, you will gain xp multipliers for your jobs and skills equivalent to: the maximum level of the skill or job / 10. This means you will learn everything again much more quickly than you did in your previous life.

  • On your 19th birthday, you have come to know the amulet intimately.

    Come on, get your mind out of the gutter. I mean, you know every bit of it. The etching is clearly a woodchuck. How much wood would a woodchuck chuck - yeah that one.

    Training every day, you are getting stronger, faster, smarter.

    You want to, no have to go and fight criminals. But to do that, you have to keep getting stronger!

  • On your 18th birthday a strange amulet materialises in your room.

    You would not have noticed it, as you were glued to your phone. Not literally glued, you idiot - this is gonna be a long day isn't it? Don't make me reach through the fourth wall and smack you! But the materialising amulet also made a noise, and as you do not want anyone to catch you with your, uhm homework, you quickly put your phone away and check it out.

    The amulet has an etching of a strange animal. A rodent perhaps.

    The amulet emits a strange glow. Reaching for it, you realise if you touch it, your life will never be the same.

    But as your life sucks, you reckon what do you have to lose? You are a physical weakling in a city tormented by a supervillain.

    Touching it fills you with ... something™. But what?



The dev:

Starting out I had 0 javascript experience. Well not 0, I used some to automate some games (Trimps being the one I spent most time on), but nothing proper. I will spend as much time as I can on the game, but I have a fulltime job and a family though so it will be slow (same goes for conversations on Discord)

The Woodchuck has been inspired by:

  • Obviously Groundhog Life
  • Progress Knight. Double credit to the dev IC for allowing me to steal borrow some of his code!
  • Endless Training & Endless Training 2. Especially the concept of training, getting stronger and fighting enemies.
  • Absorber

    • So, is this just stolen borrowed ideas from other games

    I hope not! Although Steven Wright did say "To steal ideas from one person is plagiarism; to steal from many is research." I hope that the multiple paths, fighting enemies in the background (rather than having every build require attack speed for grinding fast) at least bring something new!
